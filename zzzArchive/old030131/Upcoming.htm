APRIL 2003

12th April, 2003       5 P.M.

White Hall, Emory University

 

Ravikiran              Chitraveena

Ram Sriram           Mridangam

A child prodigy and one of the most acclaimed Chitraveena player, musician, and composer  today. He is rated among the top  instrumentalists in the world today. Ravikiran has performed in most of the major institutions, festivals and music organizations across the world. He has collaborated with specialists of Jazz, African, Pop, South American, Chinese and Middle Eastern music.

 

26th April, 2003       5 P.M.

Hindu Temple of Atlanta Auditorium

 

Aruna Sayeeram and party

 

 

Aruna Sayeeram is a disciple of the famous carnatic vocalist Smt. Brinda (of the Veena Dhanamal school).  An artist beloved in all India, Aruna Sayeeram gives frequent concerts in Europe in the States. She also sings in the All India Radio and Doordarshan.

MAY 2003

 

17th May, 2003  5 P.M.

Hindu Temple of Atlanta Auditorium

 

Sudha Raghunathan and party

 

Sudha Raghunathan is one of the most well-known female artists today.  A disciple of Smt. M. L. Vasanthakumari.  She gives concerts in both India and in Europe and the U.S. very regularly.

JUNE 2003

 

7th June, 2003 5 P.M.

White Hall, Emory University

 

Ganesh and Kumaresh (Dual violin) and party

 

Ganesh and Kumaresh started as soloists and continue perform as soloists for the last 28 years. The violin duo have released several music albums.  They have toured the U.S. several times.
