General Guidelines on CAMAGA-JAYA TV CARNATIC MUSIC IDOL competition 2012

·         The competition’s objective is to show-case young individuals living in North America and also learning Carnatic music.

·         The vocal music competition is open to children between the ages of 5 through 18.

·         Although the format for the competition is somewhat similar to the Carnatic Idol held during 2011.

·         The judges for this competition would be professional musicians visiting from India (there will be no local judges).

·         The participants will be judged on August 12, 2012 and the judging will start at 11:30 A.M. The venue is Hindu Temple of Atlanta, Riverdale. The entire program will be recorded by Jaya TV crew visiting from India.

·         Ten contestants will be selected from the Atlanta event and the finalists will participate in the event to be held in a different city (to be announced later).

·         In addition, Jaya TV also will also broadcast snippets from the performances of most other participants.

·         In addition to the finalists from each host city, a few participants from each host city may also be chosen by the professional musicians to groom them further in music for the next few years.

·         If you wish to register your students or children - the maximum number of participants is 100 – please send your registration form (enclosed CarnaticIdolRegistrationForm_2012.docx)) and the check favoring CAMAGA for the registration fee. The registration fee per participant is $150 and it is non-refundable for any reason. CAMAGA does not hold this money and it is paid to Jaya TV in its entirety. Over and above, CAMAGA is also underwriting a significant part of the expenses for this event. We request you to donate to CAMAGA to defray the costs for this event.

·         One or more members of the audience might be interviewed by Jaya TV during the taping.

·         The registration form and these guidelines  can also be downloaded from www.camaga.org
