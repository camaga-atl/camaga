CAMAGA Membership Information

CAMAGA invites you to join as a member. You or your family can become a member of CAMAGA with the following annual membership fees.

Annual Membership begins with the Thyagaraja Aradhana celebrations

 

Family membership: $150 per year

Individual membership: $75 per year

Student Membership (individual): $40 per year

Student Membership (Family) $75 per year (College students with ID and supporting themselves)

Part-Year/ Mid-season Memberships are also available

This membership entitles you to attend all CAMAGA concerts arranged during the year. Your generous support is highly valued. Membership dues encourage and support the organization of quality concerts in the Atlanta area.
