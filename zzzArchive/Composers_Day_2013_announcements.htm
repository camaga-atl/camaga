CAMAGA

Composers’ Day 2013 (Feb. 16 and 17th) – Announcement and Registration form

 

1.      If you are interested in participating in the Great Composers Day -2013, please complete this registration form.  The registration form is required from1) individual performers, 2) group participants, 3) accompanists (Pakkavadyam) and 4) individuals interested in participating only in the group singing of Pancharatna krithis and Utsava Sampradaya krithis.

2.      You can attach the registration form to an email and send it to camaga@bellsouth.net

3.      However, if you are intending to participate as a member of a group, ONLY your teacher should submit the form on behalf of the group.  We do not accept group participant names received directly from parents or from a participant in a group. 

4.      Note for Teachers: In the case of your student groups, kindly include the names of each member of the group, and denote the group (by a group number) to which each person belongs. Kindly note that CAMAGA does not decide the groups or its members; it must be decided only by the respective teachers. 

5.      In the case of groups, we request the teachers to collect the registration fees from each non-CAMAGA member of their groups and mail the check directly to CAMAGA (6011 Rocky Shoals Court, Tucker, GA, 30084). 

6.      Registration fee for non-members: Registration fee is required of participants who are not members of CAMAGA. Each participant who is a not a member of CAMAGA in 2012, must pay a registration fee of $25 (whether participating in a group, as an individual, and performing as an accompanist).  

7.      CAMAGA members: Members should have renewed their CAMAGA membership for the calendar year 2013 by January 31st, 2013. If otherwise, they should send in a registration fee of $ 25 per participant.

8.      All participants: Kindly note that the registration forms and the registration fee from non-members or CAMAGA membership fee is received by CAMAGA latest by January 31, 2013.

9.      Acknowledgement: Once we receive the registration email and the registration form, within 72 hours, we will send you an acknowledgement confirming receipt of your registration. It is your responsibility to ensure that you received an acknowledgement.  If you did not receive an acknowledgement within five days of sending in your registration, you must contact CAMAGA and get the matter resolved.  After the lapse of five days, we will not accept any further requests to include your registration.

10.  Early closure of registration: If we receive more requests that we can accommodate within the two-day function, CAMAGA will close the registration process earlier than January 31, 2013.

 

Note:  Participants can choose to sing the compositions of the Trinity (Sri Thyagaraja, Muthuswamy Dikshitar, Syama Sastri), Sri Purandaradasa, and composers from the Pre-Trinity.
