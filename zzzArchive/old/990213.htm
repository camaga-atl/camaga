Carnatic Music Association Of Georgia

Cordially Invites you to

Thyagaraja Aradhana Celebrations

 

Date : Saturday 13 February 1999
Time : 8:30am
Venue : The Hindu Temple of Atlanta, Riverdale GA

 

The Programme :

We will start with the group singing of Thyagaraja's Pancharatna Krithis. This will be followed by individual performances, lunch around noon and group singing of Utsava Sampradaya Krithis in the evening. We will endwith Arathi.

compositions of Thyagaraja only. Each participant, at this time, is allocated ten minutes. Please avoid Raga elaborations, Swara Kalpana or chitta swaras.

There will be rehersal sessions for Pancharatna Group Singing. We will inform you of the place and time at the appropriate time. You are also welcome to contact either Mrs. Usha Balakrishnan or Dr.Sriram for information.

IMPORTANT : Please do inform us if you intend to sing or play an instrument so that we can accommodate you. We request that you also us know the kritis that you wish to sing or play.
