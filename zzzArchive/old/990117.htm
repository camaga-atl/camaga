Profile of the Artistes :

Sri H. V.Srivatsan had his initial training in Violin under the late Prof. Anoor S. Ramakrishna, Principal of the Ayyanar Music College, Bangalore. Since1985, Sri Srivatsan has been a disciple of Sangeetha Kalanidhi Palghat Sri K .V. Narayanaswamy. He has accompanied several leadingmusicians including, Sri Semmangudi Srinivasa Iyer, Sri N.Ramani, and the late Sri D. K. Jeyaraman. He has also participated in the 1988 Adelaide Arts Festival in Australia as an accompanist to his guru Sri K.V.Narayanaswamy.

Dr.Ram Sriram is Professor of Accounting and Management Information Systems at Georgia State University. He had his early training in Mrudangam from Sri P. D.Chittibabu and later, had advanced training from the renowned mrudangist Sri T. V.Gopalakrishnan. Dr.Sriram has accompanied many leading artists both in India and in the US, including Sr iChitraveena Ravikiran, Sri Neyveli Santhanagopalan, Sri T. K. GovindaRao, Smt.LalgudiRajalakshmi, and Smt. Jayanthi Ravikiran.
