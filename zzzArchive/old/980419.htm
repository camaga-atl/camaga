Artist:
Dr. Natesan Ramani is undoubtedly the topmost Flute maestro in South Indian
(Carnatic) Music today. He has been giving performances for over 50 years
starting at the young age of seven. He is highly regarded for his mastery
over the beautiful bamboo instrument, the flute and is the recipient of
numerous awards and recognitions both in India and abroad. This includes
the 'Padmashri' title conferred by the President of India and the
'Sangeetha Kalanidhi' conferred by the Music Academy, Madras. He has
performed extensively in almost every corner of the world, being an
ambassador for Carnatic music. He has several cassettes & CDs to his
credit. Dr. Ramani's experience and fantastic music is sure to make this a
memorable experience for everyone in the audience.

Instrument:
The artist's flute, made out of bamboo, has a high range and is extremely
agile, capable of producing rapid succession of notes. Its tone is full and
velvety in the low register and bright and sparkling in the top.
