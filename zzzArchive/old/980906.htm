About The Artists :

Sri T. V. Sankaranarayanan is one of the leading carnatic musicians. Like his renowned uncle and mentor, Sri Madhurai Mani Iyer, Sri T. V.Sankaranarayanan is well-known for his originality, adherence to sruthi and exposition of ragas.
