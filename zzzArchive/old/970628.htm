Hi music lovers,

Recently Rajkumar Bharathi, (great grandson of Shri Mahakavi Subrmanya
Bharathiyaar), gave a superb performance at the Georgia Tech auditorium
here in Atlanta. His voice is so melodious and appealing. Rajkumar
Bharathi, rendered gem like compositions of his great grandfather, with
the same bhava that the poet must have had when composing the songs. It
was very inspiring and I am sure it would have made Bharathiyaar very
proud. His accompaniments Ravindran(mridangam) and Subramanian(violin)
were equally keeping up to the challenge,that the Jr. Bharatiyaar had
given them.

Here is a list of the songs he rendered :

1. Vanajakshi - Kalyani varnam
2. Ganapthi Thaal Karuthinai vaippom - Naattai
3. Vandhe maatharam enpom - Maya Malava Gowlai
4. Endharo mahanu bavulu - Sri Ragam
5. Ninnai chila varangal ketpen - Shanmugha priya (excellent song and
great selection of raagam)
6. Baratha desam endru peyar solluvom - Ragamalika (reminding of
"Kappalottiya thamizhan" )
7. Kannan thiruvadi ennuka maname - Begada (this was a great one)
8. Parukkullle nalla naadu - Desh
9. Chinnanjiru kiliye - Mohana kalyani
10. Hanumana mathave hariyemmathavo - Madhukauns
11. Vellai thamarai poovil iruppaall - Abheri
12. Bharatha samudhaayam

On the whole it was a great concert. I had a great time and I am sure ,
so did others. If you need tapes of the concert, contact Dr. Ram Sriram
at rsriram@gsu.edu or (770) 493-6534 for further information.

Bharathiyaar's compositions are indeed truly great and brings out
bhakti, veeram, love, patriotism through them. Let's keep that attitude
and spirit alive in us all the time..

Just another music lover like you...
