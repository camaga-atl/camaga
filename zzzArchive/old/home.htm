CAMAGA

Carnatic Music Association of Georgia

 

Carnatic Music Association of Georgia (CAMAGA), is a non-profit organization and its objective is to encourage and promote the growth of Carnatic Music in Georgia. It is also an affiliate of the Carnatic Music Association of North America (CMANA). The concert year starts with the celebration of Thyagaraja Aaradhana, usually in the month of February. CAMAGA organizes 6-8 concerts every year.
